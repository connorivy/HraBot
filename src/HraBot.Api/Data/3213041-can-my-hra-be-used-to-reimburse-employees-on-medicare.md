# Can my HRA be used to reimburse employees on Medicare?

Yes, Medicare can be reimbursed through an HRA. Employees must have Part A and B or Part C to qualify for participation. All parts of Medicare including Medi-gap coverage are reimbursable for qualified employees and their dependents.
