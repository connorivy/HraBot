# I'm feeling overwhelmed. How do I get help picking an insurance plan?

We get it- picking an insurance plan for yourself or your family can be super confusing, and it can be difficult to narrow down your options! The good news is that we have a wonderful team of Enrollment Navigators who are ready to help, and some wonderful resources on our platform to support you.

Our Enrollment team is super knowledgeable, and can help you find your doctors and prescriptions so you can choose the right plan for you and your family. You can schedule a time that works for you using [this link](https://www.takecommandhealth.com/welcome-to-take-command-health#enrollment-call).

Also, you can check out our [Employee Resource Center](https://www.takecommandhealth.com/welcome-to-take-command-health) for additional resources that may help answer your questions.

If you need anything, please don't hesitate to reach out. We're here to help!
