# How do I record HRA reimbursements on Gusto?

How to setup and make QSEHRA & ICHRA reimbursements through Gusto

## Downloading Your Reimbursement Statement for Gusto

Great news: We've made it really easy for you to reimburse your employees on Gusto!

When your automatic [reimbursement statement](https://app.takecommandhealth.com/member-portal/hra/reimbursement-statements) is ready, we'll e-mail you to let you know to log in and [download a copy of your statement](https://intercom.help/take-command-health/en/articles/3225696-how-do-i-download-my-reimbursement-statement). Now you can download your reimbursement statement as a CSV in the exact format you need to upload it directly into your Gusto payroll.

![Reimbursement Statement Download](https://take-command-health.intercom-attachments-7.com/i/o/157980006/c79bfa5131d0546c8ec5c5ea/wwVqmlEGbWMROIJKpzzwv1P3zuIDR2ySIT3NwnuasfNQwY2FBPKH_7FsMBjEXSRq0LAjMpHT7D9Yjk957jIGuSzUeLT7QWQbMQNM-h93IPg7B4HGGzKYPc30XfZltdva1Ab6_i9R)

This CSV will provide both taxable and tax-free reimbursement amounts on a CSV you can upload directly to Gusto.

## Uploading Reimbursement Info in Gusto

Once you've downloaded the CSV, log in to your Gusto member portal. On the navigation menu to the left, click Pay, then click Run Payroll:

![Click Pay](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/1145698161/fb2edc29258b6f54b0c73237/1+-+Click+Pay.png)

![Click Run Payroll](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/1145698324/f6f7b12be32cfb995066258f/2+-+Click+Run+payroll.png)

Click Run payroll in the middle of your screen then click import payroll data on the right of your screen:

![Click Run Payroll Button](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/1145698921/6aced337cdb625fe69169019/3+-+Click+Run+payroll.png)

![Click Import Payroll Data](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/1145699285/d242088b0ed0363779714923/4+-+Click+Import+payroll+data.png)

Click +Upload and choose the Reimbursement Statement CSV you downloaded from the Member Portal (or drag & drop it into the box), then click "Import payroll data."

![Upload File and Continue](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/1145699670/850c7507372f131f8c6f5410/5+-+Upload+file+and+click+Continue.png)

**Note**: You may have to add columns if you fall under the following situations:
- SSN is required if there are multiple employees with the same name
- Title is required if there are multiple pay rates

Verify that the reimbursement amounts are correct, then finish submitting your payroll normally.
