# What are the minimum and maximum amounts I can contribute to my employees through the ICHRA?

There are no minimum or maximum contribution limits for ICHRA.

Furthermore, employers can choose to offer different amounts to different classes, and they can vary rates within a given class by family status or by age. [You can read more about ICHRA classes here!](https://intercom.help/take-command-health/en/articles/3213011-what-are-the-ichra-employee-classes-and-how-do-they-work)
