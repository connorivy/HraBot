# What medical expenses are reimbursable through my HRA?

What are the eligible expenses I can claim for reimbursement?

## Your HRA Employee Packet, which is available in your member portal, includes a list of what is reimbursable under the account.

QSEHRA & ICHRA must follow guidelines set forth by [IRS Publication 502](https://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/p502.pdf), Medical and Dental Expenses. Below is an excerpt of what is included in the manual. This is not a comprehensive list but it is a great start to understanding what the IRS considers eligible as a medical expense for tax-free reimbursement.
