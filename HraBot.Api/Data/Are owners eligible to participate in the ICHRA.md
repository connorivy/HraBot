# Are owners eligible to participate in the ICHRA?

Depending on the legal setup of the company, owners may be eligible to participate in ICHRA. For example, C-corp and non-profit owners can participate, while S-corp owners are not eligible (but can deduct expenses directly). It's recommended that business owners talk to an accountant before setting up an HRA.
