# How do I reset my password?

I can't remember my Take Command Health password, or my password isn't working. What do I do?

Forgot your password? No worries! You can [send a forgot password email](https://app.takecommandhealth.com/password-reset) to reset it.

When logging in, click "Forgot Password?"

![Forgot password button](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/254920091/f47f5bca20f7b050358840e0/Screenshot+%28100%29.png?expires=1764707400&signature=c2ec6869321a3a5b7fe2fe96ca099ce1c67e4d8418df112834045f9ebfd53546&req=diUjH8t%2BnYheFb4f3HP0gFfIXY4oaQbzy0%2BC1wjCVG1pwgXl9QDs6kI8OZNk%0As5QvX5VPDql68O3fbg%3D%3D%0A)

It will prompt you to enter the email associated with your account and "Send Reset Email." After a moment, this screen should appear:

![Reset email sent confirmation](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/254929794/1f2ab82b334ac28ccbe4d34b/Screenshot+%28101%29.png?expires=1764707400&signature=ca9d1b9be27d818647399c06585dc28cb38ea892772637ddaef5f5b34e5edfdf&req=diUjH8t3mohbFb4f3HP0gL5kZYHHCOcSmiJsv4ERoe4ykIjBTgRi7YVqhNd6%0A6QN2mLwFOeafilmejQ%3D%3D%0A)

If this green message does not appear, it means you did not enter the email associated with your Take Command Health account, and the email was not sent. Make sure to try all of your email accounts, including work and personal email addresses, to make sure the email is sent correctly!

If you see that message, but can't find the email in your inbox, make sure to check your spam/junk folders! The email should come through within 2-3 minutes.

If you're sure you've tried every email address and still aren't getting the green message, chat with us in the bottom right so we can make sure your email address is entered correctly.
