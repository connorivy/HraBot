# Is Take Command Health a qualified health insurance plan?

We're not an insurance plan, but we help you, your employees, or your customers get a qualified insurance plan.

Our proprietary software can help you identify what plans are available to you. We'll walk you through a short online interview to help you discover all your options, search for your doctors and prescriptions, and then recommend a plan that we think will minimize your total costs.

Our goal is to save you time, money, and headaches and make your employees happy by taking great care of them. We love health insurance so you don't have to!
