# Can I reimburse for premiums only (and not medical expenses) with ICHRA?

# Yes!

If you prefer, you can design your ICHRA to allow for the reimbursement of insurance premiums only. For a reminder of the types of insurance plans that qualify for reimbursement through ICHRA, check out this Help Article: [What type of plans can my employees get to qualify for ICHRA?](https://intercom.help/take-command-health/en/articles/3213042-what-type-of-plans-can-my-employees-get-to-qualify-for-ichra)

For a reminder on how ICHRA interacts with your employees Premium Tax Credits, check out this Help Article: [What happens to premium tax credits with ICHRA?](https://intercom.help/take-command-health/en/articles/3213033-what-happens-to-premium-tax-credits-with-ichra)
