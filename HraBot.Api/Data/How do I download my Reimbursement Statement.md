# How do I download my Reimbursement Statement?

We'll walk you through how to download your monthly statement so you can reimburse your employees.

---

Ready to reimburse your employees for the month? Great! You'll want to reference your [Reimbursement Statement](https://intercom.help/take-command-health/en/articles/3231141-how-do-i-read-my-reimbursement-statement), where we'll show you exactly how much each person is owed in a given time frame.

Here's a rundown of the steps to take to download your Reimbursement Statement.

1. Log in to your admin portal & click on the [Reimbursements](https://app.takecommandhealth.com/member-portal/hra/reimbursement-statements) tab.
2. Find the statement within the date range you are looking to download. Click the three dots to the right to download the statement directly as a PDF or CSV file.
3. That's it! Now you can reimburse your employees.

For more details on how to read your Reimbursement Statement, [check out this article](https://intercom.help/take-command-health/en/articles/3231141-how-do-i-read-my-reimbursement-statement).
