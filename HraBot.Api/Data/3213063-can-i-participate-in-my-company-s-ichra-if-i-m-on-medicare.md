# Can I participate in my company's ICHRA if I'm on Medicare?

Is Medicare a qualified health insurance plan with ICHRA?

Yes, Medicare is a qualifying health insurance plan for employees to be reimbursed through ICHRA.

Employees must have [Part A and B or Part C](https://www.takecommandhealth.com/blog/ichra-and-medicare) to qualify for participation. As long as you have that, then all parts of Medicare including Medi-gap coverage are reimbursable for qualified employees and their dependents.

[Read this blog to learn more about ICHRA and Medicare](https://www.takecommandhealth.com/blog/ichra-and-medicare).

## Onboarding Steps

To ensure a smooth onboarding process, please follow the steps outlined below:

- **Upload Proof of Coverage**
  - During the onboarding process, select the option to upload "proof of coverage."

- **Select Medicare Option**
  - After uploading proof of coverage, you will be presented with multiple upload options.
  - Choose the Medicare option and upload your red, white, and blue Medicare card, which must display either Part A and B or Part C.

- **Complete Onboarding Steps**
  - Follow the remaining steps to complete your onboarding.

- **Add Monthly Premium**
  - Navigate to your dashboard page.
  - Click the "Add Monthly Premium" button.
  - Upload your Medicare card again and input the premium amount.

- **Upload Supplemental Insurance Information (if applicable)**
  - If you have supplemental insurance and possess a letter from the Social Security Administration detailing all your Medicare supplemental premiums, you can upload this document instead.
