## FAQ:

**Q1: What happens if my Welcome link expires?**

If your magic link expires after 60 days, you can contact your admin, who can resend the link using the Send Sign-Up Link action in the People Table.

**Q2: I didn't receive my Welcome link. What should I do?**

Check your spam or junk mail folders. If it's still missing, reach out to your admin to resend the link.

**Q3: What are the password requirements for HRA Hub?**

HRA Hub requires strong passwords that must be at least 12 characters, including a combination of upper and lower case letters, with at least 1 special character and at least 1 number. If your password doesn't meet these criteria, you'll be prompted to make adjustments.

**Q4: I forgot my password. How can I reset it?**

Go to the Sign-In page at [Take Command Health](https://app.takecommand.us/sign-in) and click the Reset Password link. You'll receive an email to reset your password, but this only works if your account has already been set up.
