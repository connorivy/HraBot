# Can I participate in my company's QSEHRA if I'm on Medicare?

The answer is yes! But there are a few details to get settled before you sign up.

## Which Medicare Insurance qualifies for QSEHRA participation?

To participate, employees need to have health coverage that meets [Minimum Essential Coverage (MEC).](https://www.healthcare.gov/medicare/) Either Medicare part A (Hospital Insurance) or part C (Medicare Advantage) qualify as MEC.

[For more about Medicare and QSEHRA, check out this blog post.](https://www.takecommandhealth.com/blog/can-i-participate-in-qsehra-if-i-am-on-medicare)

## Onboarding Steps

To ensure a smooth onboarding process, please follow the steps outlined below:

1. Upload Proof of Coverage
   - During the onboarding process, select the option to upload "proof of coverage."

2. Select Medicare Option
   - After uploading proof of coverage, you will be presented with multiple upload options.
   - Choose the Medicare option and upload your red, white, and blue Medicare card, which must display either Part A and B or Part C.

3. Complete Onboarding Steps
   - Follow the remaining steps to complete your onboarding.

4. Add Monthly Premium
   - Navigate to your dashboard page.
   - Click the "Add Monthly Premium" button.
   - Upload your Medicare card again and input the premium amount.

5. Upload Supplemental Insurance Information (if applicable)
   - If you have supplemental insurance and possess a letter from the Social Security Administration detailing all your Medicare supplemental premiums, you can upload this document instead.
