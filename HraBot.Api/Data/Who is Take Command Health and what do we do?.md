# Who is Take Command Health and what do we do?

We are a technology company that wishes to make the individual health insurance market easily navigable for the masses. The [Take Command website](https://www.takecommandhealth.com/) is a good resource to familiarize yourself with QSEHRAs, and ICHRAs. The most common questions can be answered right on this website. Look under the resources tab!

[Take Command Help Center](https://intercom.help/take-command-health/en/) has a bunch of helpful articles written by our team! It is a good resource to look through as well!

We want to stand by our name and encourage our clients to Take Command of their healthcare in a society where healthcare can be fearsome and confusing.

- **Health Insurance Guidance:** We provide personalized guidance to individuals and families looking for health insurance plans. We analyze factors such as medical needs, budget, and preferred doctors to recommend the most suitable plans from the marketplace.

- **Employer Benefits Solutions:** We offer solutions for businesses to manage employee benefits effectively. This includes generating all necessary plan documents and ensuring compliant settings for allowable pre-tax reimbursements.

- **Employee Enrollment Support:** We offer a streamlined platform for employees to shop for ACA-eligible plans, along with the option to meet one-on-one with an enrollment specialist for assistance in choosing the best plan.

- **HRA Reimbursement Verification:** We verify employee health insurance plans to ensure they are eligible for HRA reimbursement per IRS guidelines and ensure compliance in reimbursing for eligible premiums.

- **Live Employee Support:** We provide live employee support options through phone and chat during business hours to assist with any questions or concerns.

- **Compliance Verification:** Our highly trained compliance team verifies eligible medical expenses to approve or deny based on IRS guidelines, ensuring compliance at every step.

- **Reimbursement Management:** We compile all eligible reimbursement amounts and communicate back to you how much to reimburse each employee, streamlining the reimbursement process.

- **Data Security and Compliance:** We secure medical information within HIPAA guidelines and provide all necessary tax information to ensure compliance with regulations.

- **Regulatory Updates:** We stay up-to-date on all regulatory and compliance changes as they come from the federal government, keeping our clients informed and prepared.

At Take Command Health, we are committed to simplifying the healthcare experience, reducing costs, and empowering individuals and businesses to make well-informed decisions about their health insurance and benefits.
