# HRA Hub; In-Product Help

Connecting with our amazing support team as easy as a few clicks.

In our application you have the ability to easily access a way to directly call or chat with Take Command.

**Regular Hours**

Monday - Friday 9:00 am - 5:00 pm CST

**During Open Enrollment (November 1 - December 15)**

Monday - Friday 8:00 am - 6:00 pm CST

[Watch Video: HRA Hub - In-Product Help](https://www.loom.com/share/d7d26b2bc0184f12b160fbb00f2db004) (1 min)

At the bottom right of every screen in the employee and employer portals, you will see a floating green circle with a question mark in it.

![Help button screenshot](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/m61j6vi5/1203270082/4c1a5f70742ef5a4890073bee8be/eef74eb5-c686-46c8-a7cf-38eae467e0a2.png)

Selecting that will open it up and give you the options on our help options.

- Call
- Chat

## Call Take Command

Select the call option from the available contact options.

![Call option screenshot](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/m61j6vi5/1203274679/4b10edf5b3d9800bf2ffa6fd2176/image.png)

This will open your device's default phone application which will allow you to contact Take Command.

## Chat with Take Command

Select the Chat option from the available options to open the chat functionality.

![Chat option screenshot](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/m61j6vi5/1203274299/30c2d1f0532805477eca1fe592fb/image.png)

Type in what you need help with or view previously sent messages.

![Chat interface screenshot](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/m61j6vi5/1203275524/f56a4e2643b7970b3b3a1419dd7a/27e4ba37-53e8-415a-97e8-ee9c600f2598.png)

Continue your chat with our amazing support team to get your questions answered.

![Chat conversation screenshot](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/m61j6vi5/1203276434/2dd011bccdc415ad4bd52fa91bc6/df26fd39-85f3-44a9-a5df-72658cd1eef6.png)
